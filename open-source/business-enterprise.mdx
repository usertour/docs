If you're using Usertour in a commercial setting, **Business** and **Enterprise** licensing is what unlocks licensed self-hosted features and commercial use.

While the open-source codebase includes the relevant functionality, certain commercial-use capabilities are only officially available to customers with a valid commercial license from Usertour.

These features are governed by our [SAAP License Agreement](https://usertour.io/legal/Usertour-SAAP-License-Agreement.pdf).

## When you need a license

You should talk to us about a commercial license if:

- you're deploying Usertour for internal or customer-facing production use
- you need Business or Enterprise features in self-hosted mode
- you want official commercial use rights for those features

You can [contact us](https://tally.so/r/n904o1) to discuss licensing.
