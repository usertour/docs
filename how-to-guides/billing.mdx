The Billing page lets you manage your subscription, update payment methods, and download invoices. Billing is powered by [Stripe](https://stripe.com), so when you open the billing portal you'll be on Stripe's secure interface.

## Where to find Billing

1. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar.
2. Open the **Billing** section.

You'll see your current plan. Click **Manage Subscription** to open the Stripe portal.

![Usertour Billing page — click Manage Subscription to open the Stripe portal](/images/billing-03.png)

## What you can do in the Billing portal

After you click **Manage Subscription**, you're redirected to the **Stripe Customer Portal**. There you can:

- **View and update your payment method** (card, etc.)
- **View billing history** and past invoices
- **Download invoices** (see below)
- **Update billing email** or address
- **Cancel or change plan** (if your product supports it)

In the Stripe portal, click **Invoice history** to open the invoice page.

![Stripe portal — click Invoice history to open the invoice page](/images/billing-02.png)

## How to download invoices

On the Stripe invoice page you can view all past invoices. For each invoice you can **Download** the invoice (PDF) or **Download receipt**.

![Stripe invoice page — download invoice or receipt](/images/billing-01.png)

## Need help with Stripe or invoices?

- For **invoice or payment issues** (wrong amount, missing invoice, etc.), contact [support@usertour.io](mailto:support@usertour.io).
- For **Stripe account or portal** questions, you can also refer to [Stripe's documentation](https://stripe.com/docs) or their support.
