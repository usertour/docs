---
title: "Init()"
---

Initializes Usertour.js to target your company. Must be called once (per page load) before you make any other call.

## Parameters

<ParamField path="token" type="string" required>
Your Usertour.js Token, which you can find under [Settings -> Environments](https://app.usertour.io). Note that if you have multiple environments (e.g. Production and Staging) that each environment has a unique token. 
</ParamField>
